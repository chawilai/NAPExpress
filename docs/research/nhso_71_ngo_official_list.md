# NHSO Section 3 HIV Service Units — Search for the Official 71-Org List

**Research date:** 2026-04-13
**Goal:** Locate the authoritative NHSO list of 71 NGO/CBO organizations registered as "หน่วยบริการรับส่งต่อเฉพาะด้าน HIV/AIDS" under มาตรา 3 (Section 3) of the National Health Security Act B.E. 2545.

---

## 1. สรุปผลการค้น (Executive Summary)

**The authoritative, consolidated 71-org roster is NOT publicly available as a single document.** NHSO announces the 71-unit figure in press releases but does not publish a named list. No PDF, no board resolution attachment, no gazette notice that enumerates all 71 orgs has been located.

**What WAS located:**
1. **NHSO Press Release, 1 December 2024** (World AIDS Day): confirms 71 registered Section 3 HIV service units nationwide. No list attached.
2. **NHSO Board Resolution date: 6 June 2022** — this is the governing board decision that authorized civil-society HIV/STI orgs to register as Section 3 "other public health facilities." Referenced in multiple secondary sources but the full resolution PDF could not be opened (hfocus/thecoverage/matichon return 403/500 to WebFetch; wayback snapshots of matichon were readable but contain only the press-release prose, not the list).
3. **16-org verified subset (TNP+ ศูนย์องค์รวม, April 2024)** — a named, authoritative list with phone numbers and hospital affiliations. See Section 3.
4. **NHSO Press Release, 9 November 2025** (TheCoverage): the Section 3 program has since grown to **186 registered units across 6 service domains** (HIV is one of six). This reframes the "71" figure as a snapshot in time (Dec 2024) rather than a static target. HIV sub-count within the 186 is not broken out in the article.

**Bottom line:** The 71-list is not a retrievable document — it's an internal NHSO registry figure. To obtain the named roster, the user must request it directly from NHSO or TNCA/IHRI. See Section 4 for contact channels.

## 4. วิธีขอเอกสารทางการ (How to Officially Request the Full 71-Org Roster)

Since no public document enumerates all 71, the user should request it directly. In priority order:

### Channel 1: NHSO direct (most authoritative)
- **NHSO 1330 hotline** — ext 5 (ฝ่ายสิทธิประโยชน์ / ฝ่ายสนับสนุนเครือข่าย)
- **Ask for:** สำนักสนับสนุนการมีส่วนร่วมของภาคประชาสังคม (Civil Society Participation Support Division) — this is the internal unit that processes Section 3 civil-society registrations.
- **Script:** "ขอรายชื่อหน่วยบริการรับส่งต่อเฉพาะด้านเอชไอวีและโรคติดต่อทางเพศสัมพันธ์ ตามมาตรา 3 ที่ขึ้นทะเบียนแล้ว เพื่อใช้ประสานงานด้านเทคโนโลยีสารสนเทศ/ระบบบันทึกบริการ"
- **Formal path:** File a Public Information Act (พ.ร.บ. ข้อมูลข่าวสารของราชการ 2540) request addressed to เลขาธิการ สปสช. — NHSO must respond within 15 working days.
- **Email fallback:** info@nhso.go.th (general) / use the contact form at https://www.nhso.go.th

### Channel 2: TNCA (Thai NGO Coalition on AIDS) — umbrella body
- **Phone:** 02-377-5255
- **Website:** https://tncathai.org/
- **Why:** TNCA is the coordinating body for civil-society HIV orgs in Thailand. They likely have the full roster because many of the 71 are members. Ask for the member directory of Section 3-registered units.

### Channel 3: TNP+ (Thai Network of People Living with HIV/AIDS)
- **Address:** 494 ซ.ลาดพร้าว 101 แยก 14 แขวงคลองจั่น เขตบางกะปิ กทม. 10240
- **Website:** https://thaiplus.net/
- **Why:** TNP+ coordinated the registration of the 16 ศูนย์องค์รวม subset; they have the complete hospital-embedded list and contacts for the other PLHIV-led centers.

### Channel 4: IHRI (Institute of HIV Research and Innovation)
- **Website:** https://ihri.org/
- **Why:** IHRI runs the technical certification pipeline for KP-led community services in Thailand. They work directly with MPlus, SWING, RSAT, and the KP-led CBOs — they likely have the most accurate list of the standalone-CBO slice of the 71.

### Channel 5: Division of AIDS, TB and STIs, Department of Disease Control (DDC)
- **Website:** https://ddc.moph.go.th/das/
- **Why:** DDC conducts the quality assessment that precedes NHSO registration. They maintain the list of orgs that passed the community HIV/STI service standard assessment — this list IS the candidate pool from which NHSO approves Section 3 units.

### Channel 6: Self-service via NHSO Provider Registry (CPP)
- **URL:** https://cpp.nhso.go.th/
- **How:** Use the advanced search, filter by ประเภทการขึ้นทะเบียน = "หน่วยบริการรับส่งต่อเฉพาะด้าน" and filter by service = HIV. This may yield individual profile pages with hcode, location, and contact. Not a bulk export but a workable scrape target.

- **NHSO Board Resolution 6 Jun 2022** — adds HIV/STI civil-society orgs specifically
- **Registration criteria** (from thaiplus.net/2560): (a) organizational certification from a government agency (พมจ./สภาองค์กรของผู้บริโภค), (b) 90-hour training certified by Ratchasuda College, Mahidol University, (c) hospital quality assessment + Director's endorsement letter, then (d) NHSO application under Section 3.

## 6. Next Steps for the User

**Immediate (this week)**
1. **Call NHSO 1330 ext 5** — request the list verbally first. If the agent can email a spreadsheet, you're done.
2. **Concurrent: Call TNCA at 02-377-5255** — they often have an up-to-date member directory that mirrors the NHSO roster.
3. **Concurrent: Email IHRI** via https://ihri.org/ — explain you're building a SaaS for Section 3 HIV service units and ask for a warm intro or the list.

**If step 1 fails (agent cannot disclose)**
4. **File a formal พ.ร.บ. ข้อมูลข่าวสาร request** to เลขาธิการ สปสช. — 15-working-day response deadline. Template available at https://www.oic.go.th

**Parallel: harvest what's public**
5. **Scrape cpp.nhso.go.th** — build a scraper that queries by type filter and exports all Section 3 HIV units. This is the most technically verifiable path.
6. **Merge the 16 verified ศูนย์องค์รวม from Section 3 above into your existing `hiv_ngo_prospects.csv`** — this alone raises verified coverage from ~31 to ~47 of 71.

**Note on the number:** The "71" figure is from December 2024. As of November 2025 the Section 3 civil-society framework has grown to 186 units across 6 domains. When you request the list from NHSO, ask for the **current HIV-specific count** — it is likely now higher than 71.

## 7. Caveats

- The 16 ศูนย์องค์รวม above were transcribed from article images, not an official NHSO letterhead PDF. Phone numbers and hospital affiliations should be re-verified before cold outreach.
- Some of the 55 "other" units may be duplicates in different forms (e.g., RSAT HQ vs. RSAT branch clinics may be counted as separate hcodes).
- The `news_4720827` Matichon article cited in the prior research session was not re-located; a newer article (`news_4930249`) uses the same 71-figure and has been used as the primary source here.
